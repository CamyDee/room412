Welcome. My name is Aidan Teague, and I am proud to introduce myself. I'm a creative to the bone, and even back from childhood, I had an affinity for creation. The art of graffiti / tag work and chaotic expression would always interest me, as even still, these forms of expression are an incredible gateway into laying my soul down on the page.

I specialize in digital design, specifically allocating my time to logo design / brand identity, cover and album artwork, surrealist expression, and portraiture.

Currently within the last third of my time in university, I've been able to hone in my skills much tighter, and create much more thoughtful work. I am inspired by the 70/80s rock era, as well as modern punk movements and skateboarding culture. Within my work, my main objective is to create a successful composition with a controlled chaos flooding throughout.

I believe in being consistently inconsistent. To be able to apply myself in an incredibly open and emotional field is an honor, and I wear this as a badge.
